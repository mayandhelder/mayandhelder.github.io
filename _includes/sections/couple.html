May and Hélder

May Marie, a native from Dapitan City (Philippines) of the Visayan tribe, and Hélder Monteiro, a native from Maputo city (Mozambique), met online in 2018. They both were looking to give the next step in their lives and thus found in each other their missing souls. Hélder visited May in December 2018 in the Philippines and after getting to know each better, they got engagged! May finds Hélder, a fun, hard working, yet smart young man and an ideal partner, while Hélder finds May, a smart, energetic, down-to-earth and humble person, who makes his ideal partner.

May is learning Portuguese and Swedish; Hélder is learning Visayan; so marriage is the logical next step. May and Hélder are eager to start the next chapter of their lives together.
